Example Prompt for Answer Generation

This file provides simplified examples of the prompt format used in the answer generation stage.

The actual experiment used retrieved supporting documents from the adaptive retrieval pipeline.

============================================================
2-Hop Question Answering Prompt
============================================================

Instruction:
Based on the question and the two supporting documents, provide a short and precise answer in Indonesian.

Question:
Grup mana yang bermain di festival yang lebih terkenal, Cowboy Junkies atau Matt dan Kim?

Document 1:
Cowboy Junkies adalah band country alternatif Kanada yang dibentuk oleh Michael Timmins, Margo Timmins, Peter Timmins, dan Alan Anton.

Document 2:
Matt dan Kim adalah duo indie elektronik Amerika dari Brooklyn, New York. Mereka dikenal karena penampilan langsung yang energik dan tampil di berbagai festival musik.

Answer:
Matt dan Kim

============================================================
4-Hop Question Answering Prompt
============================================================

Instruction:
Based on the question and the four supporting documents, provide a short and precise answer in Indonesian.

Question:
Apakah sutradara kedua film Tanya's Island dan Madhurappathinezhu berasal dari negara yang sama?

Document 1:
Tanya's Island adalah film fantasi Kanada-Amerika tahun 1980 yang disutradarai oleh Alfred Sole.

Document 2:
Alfred Sole adalah seorang sutradara, produser, dan desainer produksi Amerika.

Document 3:
Madhurappathinezhu adalah film Malayalam India tahun 1975 yang disutradarai oleh Hariharan.

Document 4:
Hariharan adalah sutradara film India yang telah menyutradarai banyak film Malayalam.

Answer:
tidak

============================================================
Prompt Rules
============================================================

1. The answer must be written in Indonesian.
2. The answer should be short, usually one to five words.
3. The answer should not include explanations.
4. The answer should be based only on the retrieved documents.
5. For yes/no questions, use "ya", "tidak", or another concise Indonesian answer.
